The Swedish OpenID Connect Working Group

OIDC Sweden is the name of the working group that is an association of Swedish organizations that together aim to develop OpenID Connect profiles for use within the Swedish public and private sectors. The focus is on developing profiles that are secure and facilitates interoperability.

The working group does not develop any sector- or vendor-specific profiles. Instead, the group’s work should be seen as a baseline for more specific profiles.

Digg, the Swedish Agency for Digital Government, coordinates the working group and guarantees the maintenance of the group’s work. However, all decisions are taken by the working group itself.

The Specifications

The group’s work has resulted in a set of specifications that are published at https://www.oidc.se/specifications.

To follow the work, or take part, visit https://github.com/oidc-sweden/specifications.
